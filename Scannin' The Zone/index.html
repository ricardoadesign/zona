What is Zonnin'?

Zonnin’ is an app where you can explore and adventure different routes that we offer you, weather you’re a local or a tourist looking for new things to do in the Colonial Zone, you can always find new routes available on our app.

We make this possible using QR codes, which is the main element in this knowledge adventure, in every place you’ll visit in our route, you’ll find a QR code that will drive to the next place. There are six places to visit in each of our routes.

If you’re interest in exploring a variety of places, museums, restaurants & monuments, you have a arrived to the perfect place, The must-go route includes a lot of different places in which you’ll end up with amazing experiences.

If you’re interest in learning about all the emblematic churches in the colonial zone, its exact same estructure of the colonial age, and its history, then you’re in the right place, this route is ideal if you’re looking to have a great time by visiting all the churches around you.

If you’re interested in having a great time with your friends by visiting night clubs & restaurants then you have arrived to the perfect place, this route allows you to explore all the emblematic night places, by completing this route, you’ll end up with a lot of memories and a full stomach.
